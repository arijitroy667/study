Description: Its' great how a single meal can take you back dozens of years. This is one of those recipes that never seems to fail to impress. It is maggi and it may take dozen of experience to cook of these.

Prep Time: 2 minutes

Cook Time: 2+ minutes

Servings : 1-2 minutes

Recipe:

Ingredients:

Packet of Maggi, Chopped Onion, Chopped Tomato,

Chopped Bell pepper, TableSpoon oil.

Directions:

Add oil in a fry pan to cook onion in high flame. Combine onions and tomato, then add masala mix, cook for a few more minutes.Add bell peppers.
